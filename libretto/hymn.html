Hymn of Departure

The Woman
Amazing Grace, oh sweet new Law
which saved a toad like me.
I thought my pain came from my flaw;
was blind but now I see.
The dark washed off, Shiloh be blessed,
brought out my natural light!
I puzzled oft' how I transgressed.
That question was the blight.
But oh, how clear my eyes now see
my priests', my neighbors' goal!
Their cares are: rules, stability,
but not the frightened soul.
When we have loved two thousand years
we'll reason still with might
against the blinded hearts full fear
who yet think they have sight.
