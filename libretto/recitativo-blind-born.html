Recitativo: The Blind Born

Disciple #1 Look at these sinners begging at the gate.
Disciple #2 What, this blind born, she sinned in the womb?
Disciple #3 Maybe her parents' sinning caused her doom?
Disciples (all) Teacher, can you explain her fate?
Jesus Woman, you're a sign of the coming light, so go
and wash your eyes in the pond of Shiloh.
Blind-Born Woman Ah rabbi, there is no remedy we have not tried,
all failed to restore my poor eyes' sight.
Disciple #1 Why is he picking up some dust?
Disciple #2 Why is he spitting in his hands?
Disciple #3 Why is he stirring up some muck?
Disciples (all) (spoken) No teacher, not in her face!
Jesus Woman, you're a sign of the coming light, so go
and wash your eyes in the pond of Shiloh.
Disciple #1 We are sorry about all of that ....
Disciple #2 He usually is not that bad ....
Jesus We will return when your healing is done.
For now, though, we move on.
